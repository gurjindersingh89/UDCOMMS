Specialized Services for the Chorus Network

UDCOMMS holds specialized certifications and extensive experience working directly with Chorus infrastructure across New Zealand. Our expertise ensures seamless integration and reliable performance within the national fibre network, supporting the Ultra-Fast Broadband (UFB) rollout and ongoing network maintenance.

We are trusted to work on critical Chorus systems, including:

  • OFDF (Optical Fibre Distribution Frame) Systems: Installation, maintenance, and upgrades of key fibre distribution points.
  • AOFDF (Active Optical Fibre Distribution Frame) Systems: Expertise in active components within the fibre network.
  • ZOFDF (Zone Optical Fibre Distribution Frame) Systems: Managing fibre distribution within specific geographical zones.
  • Co-location Upgrades: Enhancing and maintaining infrastructure within shared data center and exchange facilities.
  • Fibre Network Deployment & Extensions: Supporting the expansion and optimization of the Chorus fibre footprint.
  • Fault Resolution within Chorus Network: Specialized diagnostics and repairs for issues impacting Chorus-managed infrastructure.
  • Compliance & Standards Adherence: Ensuring all work meets strict Chorus operational and safety standards.

Our deep understanding of Chorus's network architecture and operational protocols makes us your ideal partner for any project involving New Zealand's core fibre infrastructure.
