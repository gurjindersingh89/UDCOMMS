Our Wi-Fi Solution Process

UDCOMMS follows a meticulous process to deliver optimal Wi-Fi performance and reliability.

1. Site Survey & Assessment

We conduct a thorough analysis of your premises to identify coverage gaps, interference sources, and unique requirements.

2. Custom Design

Based on the assessment, we design a bespoke Wi-Fi network layout, selecting appropriate hardware (APs, controllers) and configurations.

3. Professional Installation

Our certified technicians ensure precise installation and optimal placement of all equipment, minimizing disruption to your operations.

4. Testing & Optimization

We perform comprehensive testing (signal strength, speed, coverage) and fine-tune settings for peak performance.

5. Ongoing Support & Maintenance

We offer continued support, monitoring, and maintenance to ensure your Wi-Fi network remains reliable and secure.
