About UDCOMMS

Universal Data Communications Ltd (UDCOMMS) is an Auckland-based team of fibre infrastructure specialists with more than 8 years of experience building, maintaining, and upgrading networks across New Zealand.

From structured cabling in commercial builds to MPO testing in co-location facilities, our technicians combine precision engineering with field-tested expertise. We are certified across Chorus infrastructure, ZOFDF/OFDF systems, and advanced fibre diagnostics including OTDR, iOLM, Fluke DSX, and end-face inspections.

At UDCOMMS, we believe in transparent communication, rapid issue resolution, and scalable network design. Whether you're laying the groundwork for a future-focused build or addressing faults in a critical link—we’re here to deliver, long after the cables are cut and connected.
