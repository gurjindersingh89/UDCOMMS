Advanced Fibre & Network Testing Services

Ensuring optimal performance with MPO cabling, OTDR, iOLM, Fluke testing, and end-face inspection.

Precision Testing for Uncompromised Network Performance

The integrity and performance of your network infrastructure are paramount. UDCOMMS provides advanced testing and certification services using industry-leading equipment and methodologies. Our meticulous testing ensures that your fibre and copper cabling systems meet or exceed performance standards, preventing potential issues and maximizing uptime.

Our comprehensive advanced testing capabilities include:

  • MPO Cabling Testing: Specialized testing for Multi-fibre Push-on (MPO) connectors and high-density fibre systems, crucial for data centers.
  • OTDR (Optical Time Domain Reflectometer) Testing: Precise fault location, splice loss, and attenuation measurement for fibre optic cables.
  • iOLM (Intelligent Optical Link Mapper) Testing: Advanced fibre characterization and troubleshooting, providing intuitive graphical views of fibre links.
  • Fluke DSX Testing: Comprehensive certification for copper cabling (Cat5e, Cat6, Cat6A) ensuring performance and compliance.
  • Fibre End-Face Inspection: Critical inspection of fibre connector end-faces to identify contamination or damage that can cause signal loss.
  • Power Meter & Light Source Testing: Measuring optical power loss for overall link attenuation.
  • Network Performance Baselines: Establishing benchmarks for your network's optimal operation.

With UDCOMMS's advanced testing services, you gain confidence in the reliability and efficiency of your network infrastructure.

Why Advanced Testing is Essential

Thorough testing and certification are vital for the long-term health and performance of your network:

  • Ensure Optimal Performance: Verify that your cabling supports the required speeds and bandwidth.
  • Prevent Costly Downtime: Identify and rectify potential issues before they lead to network failures.
  • Validate Installations: Confirm that new installations meet design specifications and industry standards.
  • Facilitate Troubleshooting: Accurate test data simplifies future fault finding and maintenance.
  • Extend Lifespan of Infrastructure: Proactive testing helps maintain the integrity of your cabling.
  • Meet Compliance Requirements: Ensure your network adheres to industry and regulatory standards.

Our Advanced Testing Process

UDCOMMS employs a rigorous process for all advanced testing services:

  1. Scope Definition: Understanding your testing requirements and the specific network components to be assessed.
  2. Pre-Test Preparation: Ensuring all equipment is calibrated and ready for accurate measurements.
  3. Comprehensive Testing: Executing a series of tests using specialized tools (MPO, OTDR, iOLM, Fluke, etc.).
  4. Data Analysis & Reporting: Interpreting test results and providing clear, actionable reports with recommendations.
  5. Certification & Documentation: Delivering official certification documents and updated network schematics.
