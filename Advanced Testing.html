Advanced Fibre & Network Testing Services

Ensuring optimal performance with **MPO cabling, OTDR, iOLM, Fluke testing,** and end-face inspection.

Precision Testing for Uncompromised Network Performance

The integrity and performance of your network infrastructure are paramount. Our rigorous testing protocols guarantee your systems perform at their peak, with precision that exceeds industry standards.

MPO Cabling Testing

Specialized testing for Multi-fibre Push-on (MPO) connectors and high-density fibre systems, crucial for data centers and network upgrades.

OTDR Testing

Precise fault location, splice loss, and attenuation measurement for all fibre optic cables using Optical Time Domain Reflectometry.

iOLM Testing

Advanced fibre characterization and troubleshooting with intuitive graphical views of fibre links, simplifying complex analysis.

Backed by Industry-Leading Tools and Certifications

We use cutting-edge technology and hold key certifications to deliver reliable, certified results every time.

Fluke Testing

We use the industry-standard Fluke DSX-5000 to perform comprehensive copper and fibre certification, guaranteeing your network meets TIA/ISO standards.

EXFO OTDR/iOLM

We use the industry-standard EXFO OTDR to perform comprehensive fibre certification, guaranteeing your network meet the standards.

Chorus Infrastructure Specialists

Our technicians are certified to work on Chorus's national fibre network, including OFDF, AOFDF, and ZOFDF systems, ensuring seamless integration and reliability.
